5 Steps to mental wellbeing

Try these 5 steps to help you feel more positive and able to get the most out of life.

Good relationships are important for your mental wellbeing. They can: help you to build a sense of belonging and self-worth give you an opportunity to share positive experiences provide emotional support and allow you to support others There are lots of things you could try to help build stronger and closer relationships:

Paying more attention to the present moment can improve your mental wellbeing. This includes your thoughts and feelings, your body and the world around you.

Research shows that learning new skills can also improve your mental wellbeing by: boosting self-confidence and raising self-esteem

Being active is not only great for your physical health and fitness. Evidence also shows it can also improve your mental wellbeing by: raising your self-esteem

Research suggests that acts of giving and kindness can help improve your mental wellbeing by: creating positive feelings and a sense of reward
